Discover Nature

Trail With Nature

Embark on picturesque hikes amid the craggy hills and lush pathways of the Greek Riviera by lacing up your hiking boots. For hikers of all skill levels, our hotel offers guided hiking trips, guaranteeing that you find undiscovered treasures and stunning landscapes.

Bungee Jumping

Bungee Jumping: With our bungee jumping adventures, you may experience the ultimate exhilaration by leaping from dizzying heights. While securely tethered, dive toward the transparent waters below for an exhilarating journey.

Zip lining

With exhilarating paragliding adventures, soar over the coastal cliffs and beautiful ocean. As you soar into the skies, take in magnificent vistas of the Mediterranean shoreline.
