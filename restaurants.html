Sea Food Restaurant

With dishes skillfully designed to highlight the tastes and textures of the ocean's riches, our seafood-focused menu is a celebration of the sea's wealth. Some of our standout services are:
Fresh Catches: Enjoy a constantly changing menu of fish, shellfish, and crustaceans that are locally sourced and expertly cooked. Whether it's grilled lobster tails or delectable scallops, our seafood is always top-notch and fresh.
Flavorful Creations: To produce dishes that are really memorable, our expert chefs mix cutting-edge culinary methods with tried-and-true recipes. Enjoy our creamy seafood bisques, crunchy calamari, and the rich flavors of our seafood paella.

Tarven and BBQ

At Smokey Haven BBQ, we take the art of barbecuing seriously, and it shows in the quality of our food. Here is a sample of what to anticipate, which includes mouthwatering sides and sauces in addition to tender, smoked meats:
Meats that are slowly smoked: Our pitmasters employ traditional smoking methods to give our meats a smokey flavor. Pick from a selection of perfectly cooked meats, such as brisket, pulled pork, ribs, and chicken.
trademark Sauces: Our trademark barbecue sauces, which range from tangy and sweet to smokey and spicy, will take you on a flavorful journey. Our sauces go fantastically with our smoked meats.

Italian Cuisine

Italian food is based on the Mediterranean diet and is full of pasta, seafood, fruits, and vegetables. In addition to pizza and coffee (particularly espresso), cheese, cold cuts, and wine are essential components of Italian food.

Chinese Cuisine

Northeastern, Cantonese, Huaiyang, Sichuan, and Shandong. Each one has distinctive flavors, cooking techniques, and ingredients of its own. Each also has ties to specific parts of China.

French Cuisine

French Cuisine: How and Why It Became the Standard... French cuisine always uses locally sourced, fresh ingredients. A few mainstays are wine, cheese, olive oil, and seasonal veggies. Additionally essential to French cooking, herbs and spices may give otherwise understated meals a richer taste.
