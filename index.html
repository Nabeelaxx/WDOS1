Welcome

The Hotel de Tranquille is the pinnacle of luxury, elegance, and unrivaled hospitality, nestled amidst the calm beauty of Sri Lanka. Travelers seeking an incredible getaway from the everyday will find refuge at this five-star hotel, where every last detail has been painstakingly designed to provide a memorable experience.

Accomodation

The Hotel De Tranquille offers absolutely opulent lodging. Choose from a selection of opulent suites and rooms, each of which was tastefully created with a fusion of contemporary sophistication and classic grace. A tranquil refuge for rest and renewal is created by plush bedding, modern conveniences, and expansive views of the natural splendor around.

Indulgence

At this upscale tourist hotel, indulgence is the name of the game. Enjoy gastronomic treats at the on-site gourmet restaurants, where top-notch chefs create a symphony of tastes using products that are acquired locally. Private dining rooms are provided for special occasions for those looking for a more private eating experience.

Blissful stay

The Opulent Oasis boasts a range of recreational facilities to cater to every guest's desires. Unwind by the sparkling infinity pool with poolside service, revitalize at the world-class spa, or maintain your fitness regimen at the well-equipped gym. For adventurous souls, the hotel offers a variety of outdoor activities and excursions to explore the natural wonders and cultural treasures of the region.
